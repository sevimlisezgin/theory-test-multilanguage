# Privacy Policy for Theory Test Multi Language

**Last Updated: January 31, 2026**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Theory Test Multi Language ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## App Overview

Theory Test Multi Language is a driving theory test preparation application that helps users study for their driving theory exams in multiple languages (English, Turkish, and Polish).

## Information We Collect

### 1. Anonymous User Data

**Device Identifier (UUID):**
- We generate and store a random UUID (Universally Unique Identifier) on your device
- This ID is completely anonymous and cannot be linked to you personally
- Used solely for tracking app usage statistics (total users, premium users)
- Cannot identify you, your device, or any personal information
- Stored in: Your device locally (SharedPreferences) and Firebase Firestore

**Premium Status:**
- Whether you have an active premium subscription
- Used for access control and statistics
- Managed through Apple/Google in-app purchase systems

### 2. Error Reports (Optional)

When you choose to report an error in the app, we collect:
- Question ID and category
- Error type (e.g., "Question Error", "Translation Error")
- Timestamp of the report
- **NO personal information is included**

### 3. Contact Messages (Optional)

If you use the "Contact Developer" feature, we collect:
- Your email address (that you provide)
- Your message content
- Timestamp

This data is stored in Firebase Firestore and used only to respond to your inquiry.

### 4. Local Device Storage

The following data is stored **only on your device** and never transmitted:
- Language preference
- Test progress and statistics
- Flagged questions for review
- Exam date (if you choose to set one)
- User profile (avatar selection, name)
- Notification preferences

## How We Use Your Data

1. **Anonymous UUID**: Track total app users and premium subscribers (for analytics only)
2. **Error Reports**: Improve question quality and fix errors
3. **Contact Messages**: Respond to your support requests
4. **Local Data**: Provide app functionality and personalized experience

## Data Storage and Security

### Firebase Firestore

We use Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud) to store:
- Anonymous UUIDs and premium status
- Error reports
- Contact messages

**Security Measures:**
- All Firebase data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Data stored in secure Google Cloud servers
- Access restricted through Firebase Security Rules
- No sensitive personal information is stored

### Local Storage

Data stored on your device uses:
- iOS: UserDefaults and Keychain (encrypted)
- Android: SharedPreferences and Keystore (encrypted)
- Platform-standard security mechanisms

## Data Retention

- **Anonymous UUID**: Retained indefinitely for statistical purposes
- **Error Reports**: Retained until resolved or manually deleted by admin
- **Contact Messages**: Retained for support purposes; can be deleted upon request
- **Local Device Data**: Stored until you delete the app or reset statistics

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Deletion

1. **Delete All Local Data**: Uninstall the app from your device
2. **Reset Statistics**: Use "Reset Statistics" button in Profile section
3. **Delete Firebase Data**: Contact us at sevimli.sezgin@gmail.com to request deletion of your anonymous UUID and associated data
4. **Cancel Subscription**: Manage through Apple App Store or Google Play Store settings

### Opt-Out Options

- **Error Reporting**: Optional - only triggered when you tap "Report Error"
- **Contact Messages**: Optional - only when you use "Contact Developer"
- **Notifications**: Can be disabled in Profile settings or device settings
- **Analytics**: We only track anonymous user counts; cannot be individually opted out as data is not personally identifiable
